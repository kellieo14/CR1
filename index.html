About Me

I am a graduate of Biola Univeristy and have a degree in anthropology. After I graduated I spent a year in AmeriCorps NCCC traveling the country with a small team of people working in areas of urban and rural development, environmental stewardship, energy conservation and disaster relief. After my term with AmeriCorps I started work for Marriott. I have spent the last few years working for them in different positions: Food & Beverage Manager/Event planner and Front Office Manager.
